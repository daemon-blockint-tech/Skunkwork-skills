# Skunk Works Key Roles — Quick Reference

## Leadership
- **Skunk Works Manager / Program Manager**: Absolute program authority (technical + financial + people). Reports directly to top leadership. "CEO of the project."
- **Executive Sponsor**: Provides political protection and stable funding. Shields team from corporate/government bureaucracy.

## Core Technical
- **Visionaries / Systems Architects**: Define breakthrough concepts and solve hard physics/systems problems.
- **Aerodynamics & Thermal Engineers**: Extreme environment performance.
- **Structures & Mechanical Engineers**: Airframe, mechanisms, advanced manufacturing/composites.
- **Electrical, Avionics & EW Engineers**: Sensors, radar, communications, electronic warfare.
- **AI/ML & Cyber Engineers** (modern): Autonomy, decision systems, resilience in contested environments.
- **Test & Flight Engineers**: Early and continuous testing authority. Design engineers stay involved in testing.

## Mission Support (Lean & Empowered)
- **Translators / Liaisons**: Bridge technical team and operational customer needs.
- **Specialized Procurement**: High authority, fast commercial-style buying, secrecy-focused.
- **Quality / Inspection**: Deliberately minimized. Responsibility pushed to engineers and vendors.

## Cultural Rules
- Small team (10-20 core for early phases)
- Co-located and cross-functional
- Minimal process and approvals
- Doers > Bureaucrats
- Fail fast, learn faster
- Performance (not headcount) based recognition
- Strong compartmentalization for speed and security
